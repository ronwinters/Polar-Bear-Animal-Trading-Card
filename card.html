Polar Bear

Polar bears can gallop as fast as a horse over short distances but quickly overheat.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Average Weight: male-990lbs, female-330 to 550lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 15 to 18 years
  • Habitat: Artic sea ice

Polar bears are large, powerful carnivorous mammals native to the Arctic region. They have thick white fur and a layer of fat to insulate them from the cold.
